📚 Tuition Rates

All sessions are one-to-one, tailored to your goals, and shaped around the topics you want to study. You’re welcome to book just one lesson, or multiple in advance.

  • A-Level Maths, Further Maths, Chemistry, or Physics: £45/hr
  • GCSE Maths or Science (any board): £40/hr
  • Key Stage 3 Maths or Science: £35/hr

🌐 Online sessions come with a 10% discount — perfect for students outside my local area or those who prefer learning from home.

👥 Group sessions available by request (up to 3 students). I do not provide students myself, but discounts can apply when learning with friends or siblings — just ask.

📦 Block bookings: Pre-pay for 10 sessions and receive 10% off the total. Flexible scheduling still applies, as long as cancellation rules are respected.

📝 Entrance Exams & Specialist Preparation

I offer coaching for students preparing for selective exams, admissions tests, and academic competitions. These include:

  • 11+ and 13+ entrance exams (maths and reasoning-focused)
  • TMUA (Test of Mathematics for University Admission)
  • MAT (Mathematics Admissions Test – Oxford, Imperial)
  • STEP (Sixth Term Examination Paper – Cambridge, Warwick, Imperial)
  • UKMT
  • ESAT (Engineering & Science Admissions Test – for Cambridge Natural Sciences, Engineering, and Veterinary Medicine)
  • UCAT and GAMSAT (Medical entry – foundation support in maths, science & logic)

Rates for entrance/scholarship prep: typically £50–£60/hr depending on the exam. Please contact me to discuss your goals and we can arrange a bespoke plan.

📌 Lesson Policy

  • Lessons are shaped around your goals — please let me know what you’d like to cover at least 24 hours in advance.
  • Need to cancel or reschedule? 24 hours’ notice is required.
  • Lessons cancelled with less than 24 hours’ notice will be charged in full.
